Discover the World of Cuisines

Explore the unique flavours of different countries and cultures from around the globe.

Choose from a variety of meals from around the world, with easy to follow recipes.

Cuisines

Italian

Italian cuisine has developed through centuries of social and political changes, with roots as far back as the 4th century BCE.

Mexican

Mexican cuisine is a complex and ancient cuisine, with techniques and skills developed over thousands of years of history.

Malaysian

Malaysian cuisine consists of cooking traditions and practices found in Malaysia, reflecting the multiethnic makeup of its population.

Chinese

Chinese cuisine is an important part of Chinese culture, which includes cuisine originating from the diverse regions of China.

Turkish

Turkish cuisine is largely the heritage of Ottoman cuisine, which can be described as a fusion and refinement of Central Asian, Middle Eastern, Eastern European, and Balkan cuisines.

Korean

Korean cuisine is largely based on rice, vegetables, and meats. Traditional Korean meals are named for the number of side dishes that accompany steam-cooked short-grain rice.

Japanese

Japanese cuisine is largely based on rice, fish, seafood and vegetables. Traditional Japanese meals often featire rice as the staple food, served with a variety of side dishes such as miso soup, pickles, grilled fish and vegetables.

Nigerian

Nigerian cuisine is largely based on starchy foods such as cassava, yam, and plantain, along with a variety of meats and vegetables. Traditional Nigerian meals often feature a starchy staple food, served with a soup or stew made with meat or fish and a variety of vegetables.

Indian

Indian cuisine is known for its rich flavors, spices, and regional diversity. It is largely based on grains, legumes, vegetables, and dairy products, with a wide variety of meats and seafood also being consumed.

Vietnamese

Vietnamese cuisine is largely based on rice, noodles, fresh herbs, and vegetables, with a variety of meats and seafood also being consumed. Traditional Vietnamese meals often feature rice or rice noodles as the staple food, served with a variety of fresh herbs, vegetables, and meats or seafood.

Kenyan

Kenyan cuisine is diverse, reflecting the country's different tribes and regions. Some popular dishes include ugali (a type of cornmeal porridge), nyama choma (grilled meat), sukuma wiki (a dish made from collard greens), and chapati (a type of flatbread). Tea is also a popular beverage in Kenya, with chai tea being a staple drink.

Lebanese

Lebanese cuisine is a mix of Mediterranean and Middle Eastern flavors. Some popular dishes include tabbouleh (a salad made with parsley, tomatoes, and bulgur wheat), hummus (a dip made from chickpeas), shawarma (thinly sliced meat wrapped in pita bread), and kibbeh (a dish made from ground meat and cracked wheat). Lebanese cuisine also features a variety of grilled meats and vegetables, as well as desserts such as baklava and maamoul.
